Our Story
In 2015, our founders discovered something startling: 60% of U.S. homes are underinsured. They done deeper and found that home insurance is sorely outdated, from the cumbersome way it’s bought and sold, to the antiquated coverage it contains, to its needlessly high prices. We launched Hippo with deep belief that homeowners deserve better, cheaper, modern home insurance.
