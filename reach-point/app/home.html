About a Platform

ReachPoint Recruitment ChatBot platform can independently screen and assess thousands of candidates per day via TESTS, and then report on suitable candidates for further interaction. Interested candidates click on the Ad link themselves and are tested by the Bot.

In this way, the system selects active candidates who are interested in the job. Pre-Employment Assessment and Screening take place anywhere in the world that you specify, regardless of the location of your company.

Project Functions:

A wide range of services and project opportunities
Automated Search for Candidates via the Facebook Ads

We use Facebook advertising campaigns to find suitable candidates for further testing. Candidates click on the Ads link and are tested in the ChatBot.

Integration with your CRM
If you are used to working with your CRM, you can see the results of the survey with the candidate card in your system. Please contact the support of the project to discuss the possibility of integration with your system.
Display of Candidates who have Successfully Passed the Test on the Trello Board

Using the Trello Board, you can see the candidates passing through the selection funnel. You can connect your Trello account to the system or get an invitation to the board in our account

Pre-screening Candidates using the Messenger ChatBot

More than 100 tests from the top 100 industries are ready for pre-selection of the candidate.

Creating and Launching Ads via Messenger ChatBot

Adding a vacancy and launching a campaign on Facebook is performed using the ChatBot. You can choose a suitable set of tests for candidates from the list or download a PDF with the vacancy to determine the subject automatically. The system will tell you which test to choose.

Thanks to our technology, creating an Ad is easy even for an unprepared user.

Process of Interacting with ChatBot

To interact with the messenger, you need to log in via Facebook. You can start chatting with a ChatBot on our page on Facebook by clicking "Send Message", or on our website.
step

Registration

The registration process begins as soon as you log in to the messenger and start answering questions. The data that the ChatBot asks you for is necessary for contacting HR and correctly placing your Job Offer.

step

How to Create a Vacancy

The process of creating a vacancy with ReachPoint is very simple. After registering, a User can create a vacancy.

Next, the ChatBot will tell you what data it needs to create an Ad and help you to choose a test.

step

How to Choose a Test

More than 100 tests from the top 100 industries are ready for pre-selection of the candidate. You can choose a suitable set of tests for candidates from the list or download a PDF with the vacancy to determine the subject automatically. The system will tell you which test to choose.

We use trained AI-powered machine learning technology to analyze vacancy text and choose the most suitable tests for candidates.
step

Selection of Results

Using your account in the CRM, you will see the passage of candidates through the selection funnel in real time: in progress, passe and failed. You will also be able to view the candidate's characteristics and assess their abilities in the Final Profile.
step

How to Contact an Applicant

We do not collect personal data. If you use Trello or your own integrated system, you can contact the Applicant directly through the Applicant Card by switching to chat mode.

Management System

By default, we use Trello as a universal task tracker for HR. Using the Trello Board, you can see the candidates passing through the selection funnel. In online mode you can see candidates, who are in process, who passed and failed the test, and the final profile of the candidate with test results and characteristics of their abilities.

You can connect your account to the Trello system or get an invitation to the Board in our account.
