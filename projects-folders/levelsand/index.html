Auditing Services and Assurance

All businesses must produce annual accounts. The nature of these documents is dictated by the type of business and its size, structure, and complexity.

A professional, external audit is often required to provide independent verification of the financial information presented in the accounts. This will provide reassurance to the internal and external stakeholders of the company — such as directors and shareholders, financiers, and regulators.

Our audit staff has a wealth of experience, including with both commercial and not-for-profit organizations, in some of the following sectors:

The audit process will identify any areas of weakness in your current practices. We will then offer practical advice on how improvements can be implemented moving forward. Our staff is altogether familiar with client money audits, variable service charge audits, FCA regulations, and solicitors rules.
